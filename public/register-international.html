How to Get There
✈️
From the Airport
From NAIA (Manila), take Skyway → SLEX → Sta. Rosa exit → Tagaytay Road. By Grab/taxi approx. ₱800–₱1,200. Travel time: ~2 hrs.
🚗
By Private Car / Grab
From Manila, take SLEX → Sta. Rosa–Tagaytay Road. CCT is along Km 58, past Robinsons Tagaytay. Travel time: ~1.5–2 hrs.
🏨
Pre-Conference Arrival
We recommend arriving in Manila a day early. Hotels near NAIA are convenient for an early morning departure to Tagaytay.
