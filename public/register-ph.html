How to Get There
🚗
By Private Car
From Manila, take SLEX → Sta. Rosa–Tagaytay Road. Follow signs to Tagaytay–Nasugbu Highway. CCT is along Km 58, past Robinsons Tagaytay. Travel time: ~1.5–2 hrs.
🚌
By Bus (from Manila)
Take a JAC Liner or ALPS bus bound for Tagaytay from Buendia or Cubao. Alight at Mahogany Market, then ride a jeepney or tricycle to CCT. Travel time: ~2 hrs.
🚐
From Cavite / CCSGM
Take the Imus–Tagaytay route via Amadeo Road. Alternatively, ride a jeepney from Imus or Dasmarinas toward Tagaytay. Travel time: ~45–60 mins.
✈️
From the Airport
From NAIA, take Skyway → SLEX → Sta. Rosa exit, then follow Tagaytay Road. By Grab/taxi: approx. ₱800–₱1,200. Travel time: ~2 hrs.
